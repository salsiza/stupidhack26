---LYRICS---

Olet itse valinnut sivun, jonka sisältö saattaa
loukata herkkää persoonaasi.
Tiedät sen viimeistään nyt kun on viimeinen
tilaisuutesi poistua ajoissa tältä sivulta.

Valituksia ei vastaanoteta.

I Was Walking Down Canal Street

I was walking down Canal Street
knockin' every door.
God damn, son of a bitch, 
I couldn't find a whore.

I finally found a whore
she was rather thin.
God damn, son of a bitch,
I couldn't get it in.

I finally got it in
worked my way around.
God damn, son of a bitch,
I couldn't get it out.

I finally got it out
it was rather sore;
The moral of this story is:
Never fuck a whore...
