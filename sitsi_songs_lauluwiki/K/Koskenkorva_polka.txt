Title: Koskenkorva polka
Category: K
Source: https://lauluwiki.fi/
Source_Type: Lauluwiki
Type: sitsi_song
Melody: Ievan polkka
Tags: Snapsilaulut

---LYRICS---

Have you seen this Koskenkorva
shining in our glasses now?
Pure as snow and white like polka
worries and troubles all be gone!
For the worries and troubles
are for them sobers’.
We’re not carrying earthly burdens
Jolly chilly hipsy tipsy trashed
and happily missing notes again!

Kossu the national drink of ours
entertains us through the night.
Much like during the olden days
it soothes and sets our minds to flight.
Oh this lovely Koskenkorva!
Drink it and head for night’s Nirvana.
Jolly chilly hipsy tipsy trashed
and happily missing notes again!

---END---
