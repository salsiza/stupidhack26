---LYRICS---

Today an empty boardroom
Now I am only passing through
When the stars in vision, mission, is to drink it all
We think of all the lengthy meetings
Among the board which I will lead
After which the Council only will say this:

Oh could we please move on
Move on
This board is heading quickly straight to hell
Still it won't move on
Move on
We never achieve much of anything 

The board is like a flock of sheep
But I can't get too much of sleep
Since as the leader I'm the dealer of the game we play
I long for simple days of past when
I could just drink, complain and play
In the Council I will face this dismay:

Oh could we please move on…
